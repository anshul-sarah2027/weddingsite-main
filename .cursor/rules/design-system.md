# Design System

## Core Philosophy

The website should feel like opening a luxury travel journal.

Never design it like a template wedding website.

Every section should feel intentional.

Luxury comes from restraint.

---

# Emotional Goal

Every visitor should feel

"I'm about to travel somewhere extraordinary."

Not

"I opened a wedding website."

---

# Design Keywords

Luxury

# Visual Inspiration

Aman Resorts

Four Seasons

Editorial magazines

Luxury books

Avoid generic wedding templates.

---

# Layout

Lots of whitespace.

Large imagery.

Editorial typography.

Comfortable reading width.

Breathing room.

---

# Typography

Editorial serif headings.

Modern sans-serif body.

Elegant hierarchy.

Readable on every device.

Typography should communicate luxury before color.

---

# Colors

Inspired by Kerala.

Forest Green

Ivory

Temple Gold

Muted Sage

Warm Brown

Backwater Blue

Never use overly saturated colors.

---

# Motion

Motion should feel invisible.

Allowed

Fade

Reveal

Blur

Parallax

Mask transitions

Slow scaling

Avoid

Bounce

Elastic

Overshooting

Flashy effects

---

# Components

Every component should answer

Why does it exist?

Can it be reused?

Does it improve storytelling?

If not, redesign it.

---

# Images

Large editorial photography.

Natural light.

Kerala landscapes.

Botanical details.

Architecture.

Culture.

No stock feeling.

---

# Cards

Minimal.

Thin borders.

Soft backgrounds.

Very subtle shadows.

Never look like dashboards.

---

# Icons

Use Lucide.

Minimal.

Consistent sizing.

Never decorative.

---

# Mobile

Every section must feel premium on mobile first.

Touch friendly.

Readable.

Fast.
