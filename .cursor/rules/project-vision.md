# Project Vision

## The Mission

This project exists to create a memorable digital experience for guests attending Anshul and Sarah's wedding in Kumarakom, Kerala.

The website begins the wedding experience long before guests arrive.

---

# The Audience

International guests.

Many have never

Visited India

Visited Kerala

Attended an Indian wedding

The website should remove uncertainty.

It should create excitement.

---

# The Experience

Guests should move through the website like reading a beautifully designed travel journal.

Every scroll should answer a question before they ask it.

---

# Story Flow

Arrival

↓

Welcome to Kerala

↓

Meet the Couple

↓

Wedding Weekend

↓

Destination

↓

---

# What Success Looks Like

Guests understand

The ceremonies

The traditions

Where to stay

How to travel

What to wear

What to explore

How to RSVP

Without asking additional questions.

---

# Personality

Warm

Calm

Inviting

Elegant

Honest

Helpful

Human

Minimal

Never overwhelming.

---

# Guiding Principle

The website is not trying to impress.

It is trying to welcome.

Everything should feel effortless.

---

# Final Feeling

When someone closes the website, they should think

"I genuinely can't wait to experience this wedding."

Every design decision, animation, layout, image, and interaction should support that emotion.

If a feature does not contribute to that feeling, it probably does not belong in the project.
